The Gonimbrasia belina butterfly is a species of ditrisid lepidopteran of the family Saturniidae (subfamily Saturniinae, commonly called emperor butterflies). It is a nocturnal butterfly native to southern Africa; it has large edible caterpillars, the mopani or mopane caterpillars, which are an important source of protein for millions of people in southern Africa...<a href="https://es.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gonimbrasia_belina">Learn more</a>
